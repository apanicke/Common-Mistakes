Common Mistakes and Things to Avoid

List of common mistakes and things to avoid:

  1. Trips are being submitted in the delegate’s name instead of the traveler’s name. Therefore, the delegate is receiving reimbursement rather than the traveler.

    • If you are a super delegate, you need to ‘select another user’, and type in the traveler’s in order to submit on behalf of the traveler.

    • If you are a delegate, you will select the traveler’s name from your dropdown list.

  2. Initiators are entering incorrect start and end dates on the trip. Be sure to check that the calendar is on the correct Month before selecting the day. Also, be sure to include any personal time taken in the trip dates.

  3. For per diem, make sure the location entered matches the lodging location.

  4. Adding prepaid lodging as a reimbursable item:

    • If per diem is requested and the lodging was prepaid by IU however not yet in the eWallet, the ‘per diem without lodging’ warning will fire and a ‘response’ is required; add “hotel was prepaid by IU” in order to submit the reimbursement.

Egencia Update:

  • For those who make travel arrangements for IU students, the student will need to have a profile/account set up within Egencia. You can no longer use your guest account to book for students. See the Travel Website for further information.
